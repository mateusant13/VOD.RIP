# VOD.RIP 🪦 — Save Twitch & Kick videos the easy way

VOD.RIP 🪦 is a simple desktop app for saving Twitch and Kick content without friction.
Paste a link. Find the moment you want. Preview it. Trim it. Download it.
No setup headaches. No command line. No extensions. Just the content you want, ready to keep.

## Save moments, not entire streams

Most tools make you download everything just to get a small highlight. VOD.RIP is built for the opposite.

- Save full VODs or just the parts you care about
- Capture clips and highlights instantly
- Skip hours of content and keep only the moments worth saving
- Turn streams into reusable, editable video files

---

## Watch before you commit

Not sure if it's the right moment? Preview anything before downloading.

Open a small preview window, check the content, and decide instantly whether it's worth saving.

---

## Built for people who follow creators

Keep your favorite Twitch and Kick channels in one place.

- Pin creators you actually watch
- See recent VODs and clips at a glance
- Switch between platforms without juggling tabs
- Start downloads directly from the list

Everything stays organized so you don't have to hunt for content again.

---

## Trim without hassle

Grab exactly what you want from long streams.

Set your start and end points, cut out the noise, and download only the segment you need.

Perfect for highlights, edits, reactions, compilations, or personal archives.

---

## Clean downloads, ready to use

VOD.RIP produces clean video files designed to work smoothly with your editing workflow.

- Import directly into video editors
- No broken files or fragmented downloads
- No extra conversion steps

Just download and start using your footage.
